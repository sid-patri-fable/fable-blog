## Navattic

<img alt="Navattic" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/bbc8da7a-b52d-4f4d-b9b0-c432c8c70bcd"></img>

Navattic is an early leader in the interactive demo space, known for its product tour software, especially favored by mid-sized companies. While it shines in promoting collaboration and teamwork, the absence of a free trial, slower setup, and a starting price of $500 per month could be drawbacks for some users.

### Navattic pros

- **Customizable templates:** Navattic offers an array of templates that make it easy to customize demos for a variety of use cases.

- **Scalable solution:** Navattic allows businesses to scale their demo creation efforts, engaging multiple prospects simultaneously—ideal for growing startups.

- **Integrations:** It integrates seamlessly with tools like HubSpot and Salesforce, while offering analytics to help optimize demo performance.

### Navattic cons

- **Higher pricing:** Navattic’s price point may be steep for smaller businesses or startups.

- **No demo hub:** The platform lacks a centralized demo hub, limiting its utility for broader marketing strategies.

- **Limited mobile experience:** Some users have found that Navattic’s mobile responsiveness is not as smooth as on desktop, potentially affecting user experience.

### Exploring alternatives to Navattic

Given the absence of a free trial, slower setup, and higher pricing, some teams may want to explore [alternatives](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/navattic-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=navattic-vs-walnut-vs-fable-best-demo-platform). With a starting price of $500 per month, Navattic’s offering can be a significant commitment without a trial option.

## Walnut

<img alt="Walnut" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/b97dbf32-27ab-428e-922d-dba6b08d5af4"></img>

Walnut is designed with sales teams in mind, offering a no-code platform for creating interactive demos for live presentations or follow-ups. Its customization options make it easy for sales teams to deliver personalized, engaging presentations.

### Walnut pros

- **Collaborative demos:** Walnut allows prospects to comment and collaborate directly within the demo, increasing engagement.

- **Personalized options:** The platform gives sales teams the tools to create tailored demos for specific prospects, boosting relevance and impact.

- **Reusable templates:** Walnut’s demo templates can be easily customized and reused, saving time and effort for sales teams.

### Walnut cons

- **No free trial:** Walnut does not offer a free trial, making it difficult for potential users to test the platform before committing.

- **Complexity for basic needs:** For teams needing simple demos, Walnut’s extensive feature set may feel overly complex.

- **Steep learning curve:** Walnut’s feature-rich platform can be challenging for beginners to navigate.

### Exploring alternatives to Walnut

Given the lack of a free trial and a higher starting price of $10,000 per year, some teams may consider [other platforms](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/walnut-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=navattic-vs-walnut-vs-fable-best-demo-platform) that offer simpler setups and more flexibility.

## Fable

Fable is designed for marketing and sales teams of all sizes, offering highly customizable interactive demos that enhance engagement and streamline the sales process. With advanced analytics and seamless integrations, Fable is a powerful tool for improving sales performance.

### Fable pros

- **AI-powered demo creation:** Fable’s Quilly AI co-pilot helps users create demos in under five minutes, generate relevant demo content, switch between languages, create brand-specific themes, and automatically generate modules with simple prompts.

- **Demo hubs:** Fable provides advanced demo hubs, allowing teams to organize and customize multiple demos, and visitors can personalize their journey with advanced branching features.

- **Deep customizations:** The platform offers customization at the HTML level, allowing users to modify, blur, hide, or remove on-screen elements for greater control.

- **Mobile-responsive demos:** Fable ensures that demos are optimized for mobile devices, offering a seamless experience for users on any device.

### Fable cons

- **Limited user seats:** Fable places restrictions on the number of available user seats, which may not suit larger teams.

- **Product replication:** Fable creates replicas of your product for demo purposes, which limits direct editing or customization of the live product.
