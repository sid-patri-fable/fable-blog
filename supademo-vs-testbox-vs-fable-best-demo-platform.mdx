## Supademo

<img alt="Supademo" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/a629387d-3741-4c1c-8912-afa7580b85de"></img>

Supademo helps users create product demos using screenshots, making it a great tool for onboarding, training, and customer support. Its AI-powered voice-overs add an extra layer of professionalism, and demos can easily be shared or embedded on websites.

### Supademo Pros

- **AI integration:** Supademo’s AI-powered voice-overs and automated text generation add engagement and polish to demos, making them more interactive.

- **User-friendly interface:** Supademo is known for its simplicity, allowing users to quickly create demos without needing extensive technical expertise.

- **Interactive branching:** Supademo allows users to design personalized demo experiences where viewers can choose their own path, increasing interactivity.

### Supademo Cons

- **Limited interactivity:** Since Supademo is limited to screenshot-based demos, it may not meet the needs of teams requiring fully interactive HTML demos.

- **Limited integrations:** Supademo lacks integration with popular tools like Marketo or Segment, which may limit its use for certain teams.

- **Restricted customization:** Supademo offers limited customization for audio and video, which could be a drawback for users needing more control over their demo content.

### Exploring alternatives to Supademo

Given its limited HTML support and customization options, teams might consider exploring [other platforms](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/supademo-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=supademo-vs-testbox-vs-fable-best-demo-platform) that offer more flexibility for different go-to-market strategies.

## Testbox

<img alt="Testbox" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/bce5d344-0faa-40d8-afb2-3cadf1894d9b"></img>

Testbox allows sales teams to create personalized sandbox environments with data tailored for their prospects. It's a popular choice for proof-of-concept (POC) demos that showcase a product’s full functionality.

### Testbox Pros

- **Real-time product demos:** TestBox offers live instances of your product, ensuring that demos always reflect the latest version.

- **Prospect behavior insights:** TestBox provides real-time analytics that show how prospects interact with your demos, helping teams focus on what’s engaging.

- **Actionable data:** Sales teams can adjust their demos based on how prospects are interacting, ensuring the most important features get highlighted.

### Testbox Cons

- **Developer-heavy setup:** Creating and maintaining TestBox environments requires significant developer involvement due to its live product functionality.

- **Limited software compatibility:** TestBox supports a narrower range of software products, which could limit its usefulness for teams with varied needs.

- **Fewer integrations:** TestBox has limited integration options, making it harder to incorporate into workflows that rely on third-party tools.

### Exploring alternatives to Testbox

With its high developer demands and starting cost of $38,000 per year, TestBox may prompt some teams to explore [other options](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/testbox-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=supademo-vs-testbox-vs-fable-best-demo-platform) that offer more flexibility and better pricing.

## Fable

<FableEmbed src="https://app.sharefable.com/embed/demo/home-page-demo-desktop-v5yq1iykpaila806" />

Fable is a highly adaptable platform designed for marketing and sales teams to create interactive demos. Its advanced analytics, AI-powered features, and seamless integrations make it a powerful tool for driving engagement and simplifying the sales process.

### Fable Pros

- **AI-powered demo creation:** Fable’s Quilly AI co-pilot helps users generate demo content, themes, and modules quickly with minimal effort.

- **Demo hubs:** Fable’s demo hub functionality allows teams to manage and customize multiple demos, creating personalized experiences for prospects.

- **No need for engineering support:** Fable is built for non-technical users, enabling marketing and sales teams to get up and running without involving engineering.

- **Mobile-responsive demos:** Fable ensures that demos are mobile-friendly, providing a seamless experience across devices.

### Fable Cons

- **Limited user seats:** Fable has user seat restrictions, which could limit access for larger teams.

- **Demo replication limitations:** Fable replicates your product for demo purposes, meaning real-time changes to the product cannot be made during the demo.

- **Fable:** Fully optimized for mobile and desktop, ensuring demos work smoothly on any device.

### Demo hubs

- **Fable:** Features advanced demo hub capabilities, allowing teams to manage multiple demos and personalize the user experience.

- **Supademo:** Supports demo hubs, enabling users to create a collection of demos.

- **Testbox:** Does not offer demo hubs or similar functionality.
