## Arcade

<img alt="Arcade vs Walnut - Arcade" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/d3c4d93c-3436-44ee-aa63-ef963e2543c9"></img>

Arcade is an entry-level demo platform, offering an alternative to Loom for creating video demos. Ideal for internal teams and small organizations, Arcade enables quick and easy production of video demos using screenshots, making it perfect for clear and concise onboarding and training requirements.

### Arcade Pros

- **Multimedia content:** Allows adding audio, background music, images, and more to keep users engaged.

- **AI-generated copy:** Saves time and simplifies the creation of initial demos.

- **Time efficiency:** The platform allows companies to create demos and training materials quickly, saving significant time compared to traditional methods like video production.

### Arcade Cons

- **Limited flexibility:** Only supports video-based demos.

- **Limited advanced features:** While Arcade is praised for its simplicity, some users find it lacking in more advanced customization options and post-production editing features.

- **Lack of interactivity:** Demos are video-based and do not capture HTML elements.

### Exploring alternatives to Arcade

The lack of HTML demos, advanced features and customization options might prompt a search for an [alternative product](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/arcade-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=arcade-vs-walnut-vs-fable-best-demo-platform), especially if you are growing your go-to-market team.

## Walnut

<img alt="Arcade vs Walnut - Walnut" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/b97dbf32-27ab-428e-922d-dba6b08d5af4"></img>

Walnut is a demo platform focused on helping sales teams create interactive, clickable demos. These demos can be used during live presentations or shared as follow-ups after sales calls. With its no-code interface, Walnut allows users to customize demos, enhancing engagement and streamlining the sales process.

### Walnut Pros

- **Collaborate with prospects:** Walnut enables prospects to comment and collaborate within the shared demos.

- **Advanced features:** These features help sales teams create personalized demos for all their prospects.

- **Demo templates:** Sales teams can create demo templates, which individual AEs can reuse to personalize demos for their prospects.

### Walnut Cons

- **No free trial:** Walnut does not offer a trial option before purchase.

- **Unnecessary complexity:** The platform can become complex for creating simple demos on a day-to-day basis.

- **Steep learning curve:** Getting started with Walnut requires a steep learning curve due to its complexity.

### Exploring alternatives to Walnut

The lack of a free trial, slow implementation, less flexibility, steep learning curve, and higher pricing might prompt a search for an [alternative product](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/walnut-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=arcade-vs-walnut-vs-fable-best-demo-platform). With a pricing that starts from $10,000/year, it can become hard to decide if you are a growing team without having the option to try the product out in the first place.

## Fable

<FableEmbed src="https://app.sharefable.com/embed/demo/home-page-demo-desktop-v5yq1iykpaila806" />

Fable caters to marketing and sales teams across various scales, providing highly customizable interactive demos aimed at boosting engagement and optimizing sales processes. Its robust analytics and seamless integration capabilities make Fable an invaluable tool for enhancing sales strategies.

### Fable Pros

- **Free trial:** Users can start with Fable's free plan and create their first interactive demo within just five minutes.

- **Extensive customization:** Fable's HTML customization feature enables teams to modify, conceal, blur, or remove elements within their demos, offering a flexible solution for all go-to-market teams.

- **Demo hubs:** Fable features the most advanced demo hub, allowing teams to compile a range of demos with various customizations. Visitors can personalize their demo experience using Fable's advanced branching options.

- **AI demo co-pilot:** Create an entire demo in under 5 minutes. Generate relevant content, apply various languages, develop branded themes, and auto-build demo modules with a few simple, straightforward prompts.

### Fable Cons

- **Limited user capacity:** Fable does not provide unlimited user seats, which might restrict access for larger teams.

- **Product duplication:** Fable operates by creating a replica of your product, preventing direct edits or customizations to the live product itself.
