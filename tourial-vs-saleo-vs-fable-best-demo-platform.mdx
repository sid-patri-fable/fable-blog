## Tourial

<img alt="Tourial" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/7764a5b8-d55d-4bf1-b7e7-f46f57fdacf1"></img>

Tourial is built to help marketing teams develop interactive product tours, allowing prospects to experience the product hands-on before engaging with a sales team. The Demo Center feature makes it easy to showcase multiple product tours on one landing page, giving marketers a streamlined way to highlight different product features.

### Tourial pros

- **Marketing tool integrations:** Tourial integrates seamlessly with popular platforms like HubSpot and Marketo, making data management and workflow processes smoother.

- **Customizable branding:** The platform offers extensive customization options, ensuring that product tours match your company’s branding and messaging.

- **Demo centers:** With Tourial, marketing teams can create demo centers that allow visitors to personalize their journey by selecting based on use case, industry, or persona.

### Tourial cons

- **Variable pricing:** Pricing depends on features, team size, and other factors, making it challenging for businesses to budget effectively.

- **Learning curve:** Building complex product tours may take time and practice, resulting in a steeper learning curve for some users.

- **Mobile limitations:** The mobile experience is less refined than the desktop version, which could hinder its usability for mobile users.

### Exploring alternatives to Tourial

With no free trial, slower setup times, and varying pricing, some teams may find it worth exploring [alternative platforms](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/tourial-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=tourial-vs-saleo-vs-fable-best-demo-platform). Tourial’s starting price of $600 per month, plus extra fees for demo centers, may lead teams to consider other options.

## Saleo

<img alt="Saleo" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/40898ba2-9960-43c5-bddd-768542540731"></img>

Saleo is crafted specifically for sales teams, enabling them to customize demos by overlaying personalized data onto the product’s interface. This feature helps create more tailored, relevant demos, speeding up the demo creation process for sales.

### Saleo pros

- **Custom data overlays:** Saleo allows sales teams to overlay custom data onto product interfaces, ensuring demos align with the prospect’s specific needs.

- **Increased engagement:** Personalizing demo content improves clarity and engagement, making presentations more impactful.

- **Minimal maintenance:** Once the demo environment is set up, Saleo requires little ongoing effort to maintain, keeping your demos current with ease.

### Saleo cons

- **Sales-specific:** Saleo is built mainly for sales teams, leaving out features that might benefit marketing or customer success teams.

- **Support dependency:** Updates to demo data often require help from Saleo’s support team, which may slow down the process.

- **Complex setup:** The initial setup can be complicated, particularly for teams with complex product environments, often requiring collaboration with Saleo’s support.

### Exploring alternatives to Saleo

Saleo’s complex setup and support dependency may lead teams to consider [other platforms](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/saleo-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=tourial-vs-saleo-vs-fable-best-demo-platform), especially since its starting price of $40,000 per year may not be feasible for smaller teams.

## Fable

<FableEmbed src="https://app.sharefable.com/embed/demo/home-page-demo-desktop-v5yq1iykpaila806" />

Fable is designed for marketing and sales teams of any size, offering highly customizable, interactive demos that enhance engagement and streamline the sales process. With advanced analytics and seamless integrations, Fable is a powerful tool for improving sales outcomes.

### Fable pros

- **AI-powered demo creation:** Fable’s Quilly AI co-pilot helps users create demos in under five minutes, generate relevant content, switch between languages, create brand-specific themes, and automate module creation with simple prompts.

- **Demo hubs:** Fable’s advanced demo hubs allow teams to manage and personalize multiple demos while offering visitors the ability to tailor their demo journey using branching options.

- **Seamless integrations:** Fable connects with tools like HubSpot, Salesforce, Mailchimp, and Pipedrive, making demo data management and tracking seamless.

- **Mobile optimization:** Fable’s demos are fully responsive across mobile devices, ensuring a smooth experience for all users.

### Fable cons

- **Limited user seats:** Fable restricts the number of available user seats, which may not be ideal for larger teams.

- **Demo replication:** Fable replicates your product for demo purposes, meaning you cannot directly edit or customize the live product during the demo.

## Feature comparison

### Free trial

- **Fable:** Offers a 14-day free trial on all plans, including a free option to create a single demo.

- **Tourial:** Does not provide a free trial.

- **Saleo:** No free trial option.

### AI demos

- **Fable:** Quilly, Fable’s AI demo co-pilot, helps generate demo content in multiple languages, create brand themes, and automate module creation with simple prompts.

- **Tourial:** Does not offer AI-powered demo creation features.

- **Saleo:** Does not include AI-driven demos.

### Type of demos
- **Fable:** Supports fully interactive HTML demos across all plans.

- **Tourial:** Offers fully interactive HTML demos.

- **Saleo:** Uses live environments directly for demos.

### Demo hubs

- **Fable:** Provides advanced demo hubs that help manage multiple demos and personalize demo journeys.

- **Tourial:** Features demo centers that allow several demos to be displayed on a single landing page.

- **Saleo:** Does not include demo hubs or centers.
