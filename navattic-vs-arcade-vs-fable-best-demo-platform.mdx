## Navattic

<img alt="Navattic" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/bbc8da7a-b52d-4f4d-b9b0-c432c8c70bcd"></img>

Navattic is one of the pioneers in interactive demos, known for its product tour software that is particularly popular with mid-sized companies. While it excels in collaboration and teamwork, Navattic’s lack of a free trial and slower setup may be a drawback for some users.

### Navattic pros

- **Customizable templates:** Navattic offers a variety of templates that make it easy to customize demos for different use cases.

- **Scalable for growth:** Navattic is ideal for companies looking to scale demo creation and engage multiple prospects at once.

- **Integrations:** It integrates with tools like HubSpot and Salesforce and provides analytics to optimize demo performance.

### Navattic cons

- **Higher pricing:** Navattic’s starting price may be a challenge for smaller businesses.

- **No demo hubs:** The platform lacks a centralized demo center, which can limit its effectiveness for marketing strategies.

- **Limited mobile support:** Navattic's demos don’t always perform smoothly on mobile devices, which may hinder user experience.

### Exploring alternatives to Navattic

With no free trial and slower implementation, some businesses may want to explore [alternatives to Navattic](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/navattic-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=navattic-vs-arcade-vs-fable-best-demo-platform). Starting at $500 per month, Navattic can be a significant commitment for smaller companies without an option to test it first.

## Arcade

<img alt="Arcade" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/d3c4d93c-3436-44ee-aa63-ef963e2543c9"></img>

Arcade is designed for teams that need quick video demos based on screenshots. It’s a straightforward platform often used for onboarding and training. With a focus on fast video creation, Arcade is popular for its simplicity and ease of use.

### Arcade pros

- **Multimedia support:** Arcade allows users to enhance demos with audio, music, and images to make them more engaging.

- **AI-generated copy:** The platform uses AI to help users quickly generate written content for demos.

- **Quick demo creation:** Arcade speeds up the demo process, making it a good choice for teams needing fast, simple demos.

### Arcade cons

- **Limited interactivity:** Arcade supports video-based demos but lacks the ability to create fully interactive demos with HTML elements.

- **Customization limits:** While easy to use, the platform has fewer advanced customization features, limiting personalization options.

- **Restricted functionality:** Focused on video demos, Arcade lacks the flexibility needed for more complex, interactive demos.

### Exploring alternatives to Arcade

Given its limited interactivity and customization, teams may want to explore [other platforms](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/arcade-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=navattic-vs-arcade-vs-fable-best-demo-platform) that offer more robust features for creating interactive product experiences.

## Fable

Fable is built for marketing and sales teams of all sizes, offering customizable interactive demos designed to boost engagement and streamline the sales process. With advanced analytics and seamless integrations, Fable stands out as a powerful tool to improve sales effectiveness.

### Fable pros

- **AI-powered demo creation:** Fable’s Quilly AI co-pilot helps you create demos in minutes, generate content, switch languages, and customize themes easily.

- **Demo hubs:** Fable’s advanced demo hubs allow teams to organize multiple demos and offer personalized journeys with branching options.

- **Mobile responsive design:** Fable ensures demos work smoothly across mobile devices, providing a seamless user experience.

- **Deep customization:** Fable allows teams to modify HTML elements in demos, offering extensive control over the look and feel of the demo.

### Fable cons

- **Limited user seats:** Fable restricts the number of available user seats, which may not work well for larger teams.

- **Demo replication:** Fable replicates your product for demo purposes, limiting direct edits to the live product during the demo.

## Feature comparison

### Free trial

- **Fable:** Offers a 14-day free trial for all plans, with a free option to create one demo.

- **Navattic:** Does not provide a free trial but offers a free tier for creating initial demos.

- **Arcade:** Provides a free trial, letting users test the platform before committing.

### AI demos

- **Fable:** Quilly, Fable’s AI-powered co-pilot, generates demo content in multiple languages, customizes themes, and auto-generates modules with simple prompts.

- **Navattic:** Does not offer AI-driven demo creation.

- **Arcade:** Does not support AI-powered demos.

### Responsive design

- **Fable:** Optimizes demos for mobile and desktop, ensuring a smooth experience on any device.

- **Navattic:** Does not support mobile-optimized demo creation.

- **Arcade:** Supports scaled-down, screenshot-based demos for mobile.

### Demo hubs

- **Fable:** Provides advanced demo hubs, allowing teams to manage multiple demos and personalize user journeys.

- **Navattic:** Does not offer demo hubs or centralized demo centers.

- **Arcade:** Offers demo hubs to organize collections of demos.
