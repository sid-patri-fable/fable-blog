## Walnut

<img alt="Walnut" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/b97dbf32-27ab-428e-922d-dba6b08d5af4"></img>

Walnut is specifically designed for sales teams, providing an interactive, no-code platform to create demos for live presentations or follow-up engagements. Its straightforward customization options help sales teams deliver more engaging presentations.

### Walnut Pros

- **Collaborative features:** Walnut allows prospects to comment and collaborate directly within the demo, enhancing engagement and interaction.

- **Personalized demo creation:** The platform offers robust customization tools, enabling sales teams to craft highly tailored demos for individual prospects.

- **Reusable templates:** Walnut provides pre-built demo templates, which can be quickly customized for different prospects, helping teams save time.

### Walnut Cons

- **No free trial available:** Without a free trial, it’s harder for teams to evaluate the platform before committing.

- **Too complex for basic demos:** Teams that only need simple demos may find Walnut’s rich features overly complicated.

- **Steep learning curve:** Walnut’s wide range of features may result in a steeper learning curve, particularly for beginners.

### Exploring alternatives to Walnut

With no free trial, complex functionality, and starting prices at $10,000 per year, teams might explore [alternative solutions](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/walnut-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=walnut-vs-demoboost-vs-fable-best-demo-platform) that offer easier setups and more flexibility.

## Demoboost

<img alt="Demoboost" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/48a64de9-3499-4776-a334-470758559c58"></img>

Demoboost is a versatile demo automation platform tailored for pre-sales teams, helping them create and manage engaging demos. It also offers tools like speaker notes and real-time collaboration, making it an excellent choice for live presentations.

### Demoboost Pros

- **Rich live demo tools:** Demoboost includes collaboration features and speaker notes, allowing for more polished live demos and presentations.

- **Multiple integrations:** The platform integrates with popular tools like HubSpot, Clearbit, Slack, and Google Analytics, making it easier to track and manage demo data.

- **Collaboration-focused:** Teams can collaborate in real-time during demo creation, ensuring high-quality and consistent presentations.

### Demoboost Cons

- **Steep learning curve:** Customizing demos in Demoboost may require coding knowledge, which can be a challenge for teams without technical expertise.

- **Performance slowdowns:** Users have reported occasional slow performance, especially when creating large or complex demos.

- **Time-consuming setup:** Initial configuration, such as setting up variables and organizing demo screens, can be lengthy, though it pays off in the long run.

### Exploring alternatives to Demoboost

With a starting price of $10,000 and no free trial, Demoboost’s learning curve and performance issues might drive teams to explore [other platforms](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/demoboost-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=walnut-vs-demoboost-vs-fable-best-demo-platform) that offer easier setups and more affordable pricing.

## Fable

<FableEmbed src="https://app.sharefable.com/embed/demo/home-page-demo-desktop-v5yq1iykpaila806" />

Fable is a customizable platform built for marketing and sales teams, providing interactive demos with advanced analytics, AI-driven features, and seamless integrations to streamline the sales process and boost engagement.

### Fable Pros

- **AI-powered demo creation:** Fable’s Quilly AI co-pilot helps users quickly generate demo content, themes, and modules with just a few simple prompts.

- **Demo hubs:** Fable offers sophisticated demo hub functionality, allowing teams to manage multiple demos and provide personalized experiences for prospects.

- **Wide integration support:** Fable integrates with tools like HubSpot, Salesforce, Mailchimp, and Pipedrive, making it easy to manage and track demo data.

- **Advanced customization:** Fable enables users to edit, hide, blur, or remove on-screen HTML elements, providing deep customization for various go-to-market strategies.

### Fable Cons

- **Limited user seats:** Fable restricts the number of available user seats, which may limit access for larger teams.

- **Demo replication limitations:** Fable replicates your product for demo purposes, meaning you can’t make real-time edits to the product during the demo.
