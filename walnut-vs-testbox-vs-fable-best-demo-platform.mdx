## Walnut

<img alt="Walnut" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/b97dbf32-27ab-428e-922d-dba6b08d5af4"></img>

Walnut is designed specifically for sales teams, offering a no-code platform to create interactive demos for live presentations or follow-up communications. With easy-to-use customization features, Walnut helps make sales presentations more engaging and streamlined.

### Walnut pros

- **Collaborative features:** Walnut allows prospects to comment and collaborate directly within the demo, enhancing interaction and engagement.

- **Personalized demos:** The platform provides robust customization options for creating tailored demos that resonate with individual prospects.

- **Reusable templates:** Walnut offers reusable demo templates, allowing sales reps to quickly adjust and personalize demos for different prospects, saving time.

### Walnut cons

- **No free trial available:** Without a free trial, it can be challenging for teams to test the platform before committing to a plan.

- **Too complex for simple needs:** For teams needing basic demos, Walnut’s extensive feature set may feel overwhelming.

- **Steep learning curve:** The platform’s broad capabilities result in a learning curve that can be difficult for beginners to overcome.

### Exploring alternatives to Walnut

With no free trial, complex features, and pricing starting at $10,000 per year, teams might consider exploring [alternative platforms](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/walnut-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=walnut-vs-testbox-vs-fable-best-demo-platform) that offer simpler setups and more flexibility.

## Testbox

<img alt="Testbox" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/bce5d344-0faa-40d8-afb2-3cadf1894d9b"></img>

TestBox helps sales teams create personalized sandbox environments filled with relevant data for their prospects. It’s particularly useful for teams needing proof-of-concept (POC) demos to showcase the depth of their product’s capabilities.

### Testbox pros

- **Live product demos:** Since TestBox operates as a live instance of your product, your demo always stays up-to-date with the latest version of your software.

- **Real-time analytics:** TestBox provides advanced analytics, offering insights into how prospects interact with your demo and highlighting key areas of interest.

- **Data-driven insights:** With actionable data on user behavior, TestBox enables sales teams to refine their demos to focus on what matters most to potential buyers.

### Testbox cons

- **Developer-heavy setup:** Creating and maintaining TestBox requires significant developer involvement, as it runs live versions of your product.

- **Limited compatibility:** TestBox supports fewer types of software, which can be a constraint for teams with varied product offerings.

- **Fewer integration options:** TestBox lacks robust integration capabilities, complicating workflows that involve third-party applications.

### Exploring alternatives to Testbox

Given the high demand for developer resources and a starting price of $38,000 per year, teams might look into more flexible [Testbox alternatives](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/testbox-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=walnut-vs-testbox-vs-fable-best-demo-platform) that better fit their needs.

## Fable

<FableEmbed src="https://app.sharefable.com/embed/demo/home-page-demo-desktop-v5yq1iykpaila806" />

Fable is a highly customizable platform designed for marketing and sales teams who want to create interactive demos. With advanced analytics, AI-driven features, and seamless integrations, Fable makes it easy to engage prospects and simplify the sales process.

### Fable pros

- **AI-powered demo creation:** Fable’s Quilly AI co-pilot assists users in quickly generating demo content, themes, and modules with just a few prompts.

- **Demo hub capabilities:** Fable offers advanced demo hub functionality, allowing users to manage and customize multiple demos for personalized user experiences.

- **Extensive integration options:** Fable integrates with popular platforms like HubSpot, Salesforce, Mailchimp, and Pipedrive, making it easy to manage and track demo data.

- **Deep customization:** Fable allows users to edit, blur, hide, or remove on-screen HTML elements, offering a high level of customization for different go-to-market needs.

### Fable cons

- **Limited user access:** Fable restricts the number of user seats available, which can be a limitation for larger teams.

- **Product replication limitations:** Fable replicates your product for demo purposes, meaning live edits to the product during the demo are not possible.
