## Supademo

<img alt="Supademo" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/a629387d-3741-4c1c-8912-afa7580b85de"></img>

Supademo allows users to create product demos with screenshots, ideal for improving onboarding, training, and customer support. The platform uses AI-powered voice-overs, adding a layer of engagement and making demos easily shareable or embeddable on websites.

### Supademo Pros

- **AI integration:** Supademo’s AI-powered features, including voiceovers and automated text generation, enhance demos, making them more engaging and professional.

- **Easy to use:** Known for its simple and intuitive interface, Supademo allows users to create demos quickly without a steep learning curve.

- **Interactive demo branching:** Users can create personalized demo experiences by allowing viewers to select their own path, improving interactivity.

### Supademo Cons

- **Limited flexibility:** Supademo only supports screenshot-based demos, which might not fulfill all user needs for a fully interactive experience.

- **Limited integrations:** The platform does not integrate with popular marketing tools like Marketo or Segment, potentially limiting its usefulness for some teams.

- **Restricted customization:** Supademo offers limited audio and video customization, which may be restrictive for users who need more personalized demo content.

### Exploring alternatives to Supademo

The lack of HTML demo support, alongside limited customization and advanced features, might prompt teams to consider [alternative platforms](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/supademo-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=supademo-vs-reprise-vs-fable-best-demo-platform), especially if they are looking for greater flexibility in their go-to-market strategies.

## Reprise

<img alt="Reprise" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/1594d69f-a896-4f52-a5f9-580978774c1b"></img>

Reprise stands out for its SOC-2 certification, making it a preferred choice for enterprise-level sales teams needing secure and tailored demo environments. With its flagship product, Reveal, Reprise enables sales teams to build customized demos, providing a scalable alternative to traditional proof-of-concept methods.

### Reprise Pros

- **Robust APIs:** Reprise’s API capabilities allow for highly customized demos tailored to your company’s specific needs.

- **Enterprise-grade security:** Reprise is compliant with SOC2, GDPR, and CCPA standards, ensuring that data protection and regulatory compliance are top priorities.

- **Offline demo capabilities:** The platform allows for offline demos, making it possible to present product demos without internet access—ideal for events or trade shows.

### Reprise Cons

- **High development demand:** Implementing Reprise requires significant development resources, which can make the platform more costly and complex to set up and maintain.

- **Limited mobile optimization:** Reprise is mainly designed for web-based applications and has limited support for mobile app demos, which could be a drawback for mobile-focused businesses.

- **Integration difficulties:** Some users report challenges with exporting demos and integrating Reprise with tools like HubSpot, which often requires additional development efforts.

### Exploring alternatives to Reprise

Given the complex setup, limited flexibility, and high starting price of $30,000 per year, many teams may look for [alternative platforms](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/reprise-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=supademo-vs-reprise-vs-fable-best-demo-platform) that offer more cost-effective and scalable solutions.

## Fable

<FableEmbed src="https://app.sharefable.com/embed/demo/home-page-demo-desktop-v5yq1iykpaila806" />

Fable is a versatile platform designed for marketing and sales teams of all sizes, offering fully customizable interactive demos to enhance engagement and improve sales processes. With its advanced analytics and seamless integration capabilities, Fable is a powerful tool for driving performance and optimizing customer interactions.

### Fable Pros

- **Free to start:** Fable offers a free plan that lets users create their first interactive demo in just five minutes.

- **Mobile responsiveness:** Fable ensures that demos are optimized for mobile devices, offering a seamless experience on all platforms.

- **AI demo co-pilot:** Fable’s AI-powered co-pilot, Quilly, enables users to generate demo content in minutes, customize themes, and create modules using simple prompts, all while supporting multiple languages.

- **Demo hubs:** Fable provides one of the most advanced demo hub solutions available, allowing teams to manage a collection of demos with extensive customization options. Visitors can personalize their demo journey with advanced branching features.

### Fable Cons

- **Limited user seats:** Fable restricts the number of available user seats, which may be a challenge for larger teams needing broader access.

- **Product replication:** Fable creates a replica of your product for demo purposes, limiting the ability to directly edit or customize the live product.

## Feature comparison

### Free trial 

- **Fable:** Offers a 14-day free trial across all plans, along with a free plan to create one demo.

- **Supademo:** Provides a free trial for all plans, similar to Fable.

- **Reprise:** Reprise does not offer a free trial.

### AI demos

- **Fable:** Quilly, Fable’s AI co-pilot, allows users to create demo content, generate themes, and customize modules in multiple languages using simple prompts.

- **Supademo:** Supademo’s AI-powered demos simplify the creation of voiceovers for more polished presentations.

- **Reprise:** Currently, Reprise does not offer AI-powered demos.

### Responsive design

- **Fable:** Demos are optimized for all devices, ensuring a seamless experience on both mobile and desktop platforms.

- **Supademo:** As screenshot-based demos, they can be scaled down for use on mobile devices.

- **Reprise:** The responsiveness depends on how well the original product supports mobile, as Reprise does not directly handle this aspect.

### Setup time

- **Fable:** No setup time required; users can sign up and create their first demo immediately.

- **Supademo:** Offers instant sign-up and demo creation with zero setup time.

- **Reprise:** Requires a few weeks for setup, as it involves significant input from both the Reprise and engineering teams.
