## Tourial

<img alt="Tourial" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/7764a5b8-d55d-4bf1-b7e7-f46f57fdacf1"></img>

Tourial is designed to help marketing teams create interactive product tours, allowing prospects to experience the product firsthand before engaging with a sales team. Its Demo Center feature allows marketers to display multiple product tours on a single landing page, helping to showcase different product aspects in a streamlined manner.

### Tourial pros

- **Marketing tool integrations:** Tourial seamlessly integrates with popular tools like HubSpot and Marketo, allowing marketing teams to manage data and workflows efficiently.

- **Customizable branding:** The platform offers robust customization options, ensuring product tours align with the company’s brand identity and messaging.

- **Demo centers:** Tourial lets marketing teams create demo centers, offering visitors personalized experiences by selecting their journey based on use case, industry, or persona.

### Tourial cons

- **Variable pricing:** Pricing varies based on features, team size, and other factors, making it more difficult for businesses to plan their budget.

- **Learning curve:** Users may face a learning curve when building complex and detailed tours, requiring extra time and effort.

- **Mobile experience:** The mobile version is less polished compared to its desktop counterpart, which may limit usability for mobile-focused audiences.

### Exploring alternatives to Tourial

With no free trial, slower setup times, and variable pricing, some teams may find it helpful to explore [alternative platforms](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/tourial-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=tourial-vs-testbox-vs-fable-best-demo-platform). Tourial’s starting price of $600 per month and additional fees for demo centers might also push teams to seek other options before committing.

## Testbox

<img alt="Testbox" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/bce5d344-0faa-40d8-afb2-3cadf1894d9b"></img>

TestBox enables sales teams to create personalized sandbox environments, preloaded with data that’s tailored for their prospects. It's ideal for teams that need to build proof-of-concept (POC) demos that highlight the full capabilities of their product.

### Testbox pros

- **Live demos:** As a live instance of your product, TestBox ensures that demos are always up to date with your latest product version.

- **Real-time prospect analytics:** TestBox provides advanced analytics that track how prospects interact with your demo, revealing insights into what engages them most.

- **Actionable insights:** By analyzing prospect behavior, TestBox helps sales teams refine their demos and focus on what resonates with potential buyers.

### Testbox cons

- **Developer-intensive setup:** TestBox requires significant developer involvement to set up and maintain, as it runs as a live product instance.

- **Limited software compatibility:** The platform currently supports a narrower range of software, limiting its use for businesses with diverse product offerings.

- **Fewer integrations:** TestBox lacks robust integration options, which may complicate demonstrating workflows involving third-party applications.

### Exploring alternatives to Testbox

With a high reliance on developer resources and a starting cost of $38,000 per year, TestBox’s requirements may lead teams to explore more budget-friendly and flexible [Testbox alternatives](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/testbox-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=tourial-vs-testbox-vs-fable-best-demo-platform).

## Fable

<FableEmbed src="https://app.sharefable.com/embed/demo/home-page-demo-desktop-v5yq1iykpaila806" />

Fable is a flexible and powerful demo platform built for marketing and sales teams of all sizes. It allows users to create highly customizable interactive demos, which help increase engagement and streamline the sales process. Fable also offers advanced analytics and seamless integration capabilities, making it an essential tool for boosting sales performance.

### Fable pros

- **Free to start:** Fable offers a free plan, allowing users to create their first interactive demo within minutes.

- **AI-powered co-pilot:** Fable’s AI co-pilot, Quilly, assists in generating demo content, customizing themes, supporting multiple languages, and creating modules with simple prompts.

- **No engineering support required:** Built for marketing and sales teams, Fable removes the need for engineering involvement, enabling quick and independent setup.

- **Advanced demo hubs:** Fable’s sophisticated demo hubs let teams manage collections of demos, providing visitors with personalized experiences through advanced branching options.

### Fable cons

- **Limited user access:** Fable restricts the number of available user seats, which may pose a challenge for larger teams.

- **Demo replication:** Instead of editing the live product, Fable uses a replica for demo purposes, which may not meet all users’ needs.

## Feature comparison

### Free trial

- **Fable:** Offers a 14-day free trial across all plans and includes a free plan to create one demo.

- **Tourial:** No free trial available.

- **Testbox:** Does not offer a free trial.

### AI demos

- **Fable:** With Quilly, users can create demo content, themes, and modules in multiple languages using simple prompts.

- **Tourial:** Does not offer AI-driven demo creation features.

- **Testbox:** Does not currently provide AI-powered demos.

### Type of demos

- **Fable:** Allows fully interactive HTML demos across all plans.

- **Tourial:** Supports fully interactive HTML demos for all plans.

- **Testbox:** Uses live product environments directly for demos.

### Demo hubs

- **Fable:** Smart demo hubs let you manage demo collections, offering flexible customization.

- **Tourial:** Supports demo centers that allow multiple demos to be showcased on a single landing page.

- **Testbox:** Does not provide demo hubs or centers.

## Conclusion

Selecting the right interactive demo platform means aligning its features with your team’s specific goals. While Tourial and Testbox each have their own advantages, Fable’s advanced capabilities, customizability, and seamless integrations make it a standout option for businesses looking to create impactful, personalized demos.
