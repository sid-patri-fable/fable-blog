## Tourial

<img alt="Tourial vs Supademo - Tourial" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/7764a5b8-d55d-4bf1-b7e7-f46f57fdacf1"></img>

Tourial is a platform that allows marketing teams to create interactive product tours, helping prospects experience a product before engaging in a sales conversation. Marketers can also use Tourial’s Demo Center to showcase multiple product tours on a single landing page, making it easier to highlight various aspects of their products in one place.

### Tourial Pros

- **Create demo centers**: Marketing teams can allow website visitors to choose their own journey based like use case, industry, and persona.

- **Integrates with marketing tools**: Tourial integrates with the likes of HubSpot, Marketo, among others to make it easy for marketing teams to push data to their preferred tools.

- **Customization**: Tourial provides flexible options for branding and customizing tours, ensuring that the tours align with the company’s visual identity.

### Tourial Cons

- **No fixed pricing**: Tourial’s pricing is variable, depending on the features you need, your team size, and other factors.

- **Learning curve**: There can be a slight learning curve when first using the platform, especially for creating more complex tours.

- **Mobile experience**: The mobile experience of Tourial is not as polished as its desktop counterpart, which can be a drawback for users accessing tours on mobile devices.

### Exploring alternatives to Tourial

However, the lack of a free trial, slow implementation, and higher pricing might prompt a search for an [alternative product](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/tourial-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=tourial-vs-supademo-vs-fable-choosing-the-best-demo-tool). With a pricing that starts from $600/month and an additional subscription fee for their demo centers, it can become hard to take a decision even without having an option to try the product out in the first place.

## Supademo

<img alt="Tourial vs Supademo - Supademo" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/a629387d-3741-4c1c-8912-afa7580b85de"></img>

Supademo offers users a way to create product demos using screenshots. It helps teams enhance product onboarding, training, and customer support by creating engaging, AI-powered voice-overs that can be easily shared or embedded on websites.

### Supademo Pros

- **AI Integration**: Features such as AI voiceovers and automated text generation enhance the demo experience, allowing for more engaging and professional presentations.

- **Demo branching**: Users can choose their own demo journey.

- **Ease of use**: Supademo is praised for its straightforward, intuitive interface that allows users to start creating demos without a steep learning curve.

### Supademo Cons

- **Limited flexibility**: Only offers screenshot-based demos.

- **Limited integrations**: Does not integrate with tools like Marketo or Segment.

- **Minimal customizations**: Does not capture HTML and CSS elements.

### Exploring alternatives to Supademo

However, the lack of HTML demos, advanced features and customization options might prompt a search for an [alternative product](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/supademo-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=tourial-vs-supademo-vs-fable-choosing-the-best-demo-tool) especially if you are growing your go-to-market team.

## Fable

<FableEmbed src="https://app.sharefable.com/embed/demo/home-page-demo-desktop-v5yq1iykpaila806" />

Fable empowers businesses to create tailored interactive demos that captivate audiences and optimize sales processes. With its sophisticated analytics and seamless integration capabilities, Fable provides marketing and sales teams with the tools they need to enhance engagement and drive impactful results.

### Fable Pros

- **Free to start**: With Fable's free plan, users can create their first interactive demo in just 5 minutes.

- **Deep customizations**: Fable's on-screen HTML customization allows teams to edit, hide, blur, or delete any element in their demo, making it a versatile tool for all go-to-market teams.

- **Demo hubs**: Fable boasts the most advanced demo hub product on the market, allowing teams to create a collection of demos with multiple customizations. Visitors can personalize their demo journey with Fable's advanced branching.

### Fable Cons

- **Limited user seats**: Fable does not offer unlimited user seats.

- **Product replication**: Fable replicates your product, preventing direct edits or customizations to the live version.
