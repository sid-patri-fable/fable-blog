## Arcade

<img alt="Arcade" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/d3c4d93c-3436-44ee-aa63-ef963e2543c9"></img>

Arcade is a basic demo platform designed for teams that need to create video demos using screenshots. It’s an easy-to-use solution, ideal for producing simple video content for onboarding and training. Arcade is often compared to Loom for its straightforward video creation process.

### Arcade pros

- **Multimedia features:** Arcade allows users to add audio, background music, and images to make demos more engaging for viewers.

- **AI-generated content:** The platform helps speed up demo creation by utilizing AI to generate written content for your videos.

- **Quick demo creation:** Arcade enables teams to produce demos and training materials quickly, offering a time-efficient alternative to traditional video production.

### Arcade cons

- **Lack of interactivity:** Arcade supports only video-based demos, making it less ideal for teams that need interactive, HTML-based demos.

- **Limited customization options:** While simple to use, Arcade lacks advanced customization and post-production editing capabilities.

- **Basic functionality:** The platform focuses on video creation, limiting flexibility in creating demos that involve interactive elements like HTML.

### Exploring alternatives to Arcade

Due to its limited functionality and lack of advanced features, teams might explore [alternative platforms](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/arcade-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=arcade-vs-reprise-vs-fable-best-demo-platform) that offer greater interactivity and customization for more complex use cases.

## Reprise

<img alt="Reprise" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/1594d69f-a896-4f52-a5f9-580978774c1b"></img>

Reprise stands out for its SOC-2 certification, making it a trusted choice for enterprise sales teams seeking secure, customized demo environments. Its flagship product, Reveal, allows teams to create tailored demos, offering a scalable alternative to traditional proof-of-concept approaches.

### Reprise pros

- **Custom APIs:** Reprise’s API capabilities allow teams to build highly customized demos to meet specific business needs.

- **Enterprise-grade security:** With SOC2, GDPR, and CCPA compliance, Reprise ensures robust data security and regulatory compliance.

- **Offline demo support:** Reprise allows teams to present demos offline, making it ideal for use at events and trade shows where internet access might be limited.

### Reprise cons

- **High development demands:** Implementing Reprise requires significant developer resources, making it complex and costly to maintain.

- **Limited mobile support:** Reprise is primarily designed for web-based applications and offers limited optimization for mobile app demos.

- **Integration challenges:** Some users have reported difficulties with demo exports and integrating with platforms like HubSpot, often requiring additional development work.

### Exploring alternatives to Reprise

Given the high setup complexity and starting cost of $30,000/year, teams may consider [alternative platforms](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/reprise-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=arcade-vs-reprise-vs-fable-best-demo-platform) that offer more scalability and ease of use.

## Fable

Fable is a flexible platform designed for marketing and sales teams of all sizes. It offers powerful, customizable interactive demos that improve engagement and streamline sales processes. Fable stands out for its advanced analytics and seamless integration capabilities, helping teams enhance sales performance and customer engagement.

### Fable pros

- **Free to start:** Fable provides a free plan, enabling users to create their first interactive demo in under five minutes.

- **Mobile-friendly demos:** Fable ensures that all demos are optimized for mobile devices, delivering a seamless experience on any platform.

- **Advanced demo hubs:** Fable’s demo hubs allow teams to manage collections of demos with extensive customization options, enabling prospects to navigate personalized demo journeys.

- **AI-powered co-pilot:** Fable’s AI co-pilot, Quilly, helps create demo content quickly, supports multiple languages, and customizes themes using simple prompts.

### Fable cons

- **Limited user seats:** Fable restricts the number of available user seats, which could pose challenges for larger teams.

- **Demo creation constraints:** Fable creates a replica of your product for demos, meaning you can’t edit the live product directly during the demo process.

## Feature comparison

### Free trial

- **Fable:** Offers a 14-day free trial across all plans and includes a free plan for creating one demo.

- **Arcade:** Provides a free trial to allow users to test the platform before committing.

- **Reprise:** Does not offer a free trial option.

### AI demos

- **Fable:** Quilly, Fable’s AI-powered co-pilot, allows you to create content, customize themes, and generate modules in multiple languages.

- **Arcade:** Does not support AI-driven demo creation.

- **Reprise:** Currently does not offer AI-powered demo creation.

### Responsive design

- **Fable:** Fully optimized for all devices, ensuring demos work smoothly on mobile and desktop platforms.

- **Arcade:** Supports scaled-down screenshot-based demos for mobile use.

- **Reprise:** The responsiveness of demos depends on the product’s original design.

### Demo hubs

- **Fable:** Features smart demo hubs, enabling teams to create and manage collections of demos based on different factors.

- **Arcade:** Allows for the creation of demo collections through its demo hub feature.

- **Reprise:** Does not offer demo hub functionality.
