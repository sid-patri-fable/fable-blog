## Reprise

<img alt="Reprise" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/1594d69f-a896-4f52-a5f9-580978774c1b"></img>

Reprise is known for its SOC-2 certification, making it an ideal choice for enterprise-level sales teams that require secure, customized demo environments. With its flagship product, Reveal, Reprise allows teams to build tailored demos, offering a scalable alternative to traditional proof-of-concept methods.

### Reprise pros

- **Robust APIs:** Reprise offers API functionality that enables highly customizable demos, designed to meet your company’s specific needs.

- **Enterprise-grade security:** With SOC2, GDPR, and CCPA compliance, Reprise prioritizes data protection and regulatory standards.

- **Offline demo functionality:** Reprise allows for offline demos, meaning you can showcase product demos without internet access—perfect for events or trade shows.

### Reprise cons

- **Significant development resources required:** Reprise requires considerable development resources for implementation, making it more complex and costly to set up and maintain.

- **Limited mobile optimization:** Reprise is largely built for web-based applications, offering limited support for mobile app demos, which may be a drawback for businesses focused on mobile.

- **Integration challenges:** Some users experience issues with demo exports and integrating Reprise with tools like HubSpot, often requiring additional development work.

### Exploring alternatives to Reprise

Due to the complex setup, limited flexibility, and a starting price of $30,000 per year, teams may explore [alternative platforms](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/reprise-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=reprise-vs-saleo-vs-fable-best-demo-platform) that offer more cost-effective, scalable solutions.

## Saleo

<img alt="Saleo" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/40898ba2-9960-43c5-bddd-768542540731"></img>

Saleo is a sales-focused demo platform that allows teams to overlay custom data onto their product interfaces, making demos more relevant to specific prospects and enhancing engagement. This method helps to cut down on demo prep time while delivering personalized sales presentations.

### Saleo pros

- **Customization:** Saleo lets teams layer custom data over their product, tailoring demos to the needs of individual prospects.

- **Data-driven engagement:** The ability to personalize data for each prospect makes demos more engaging and easier for buyers to understand.

- **Maintenance ease:** Once the environment is set up, Saleo is relatively easy to maintain compared to other demo tools, ensuring your demos stay up to date.

### Saleo cons

- **Limited to sales teams:** Saleo is primarily designed for sales teams, with fewer features that support marketing or customer success needs.

- **Dependency on support for updates:** Modifying demo data requires assistance from Saleo’s support team, which may delay demo updates.

- **Complex setup:** The initial setup of Saleo can be difficult, particularly for complex demo environments, requiring close coordination with the Saleo team.

### Exploring alternatives to Saleo

Due to its lengthy setup and reliance on ongoing updates, many teams might find it beneficial to explore [other platforms](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/saleo-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=reprise-vs-saleo-vs-fable-best-demo-platform). Additionally, with a starting price of $40,000 per year, Saleo may not be the best fit for smaller or growing teams.

## Fable

<FableEmbed src="https://app.sharefable.com/embed/demo/home-page-demo-desktop-v5yq1iykpaila806" />

Fable is designed for marketing and sales teams of all sizes, offering highly customizable interactive demos that boost engagement and streamline sales processes. With advanced analytics and seamless integrations, Fable is a powerful solution for enhancing customer interactions and driving performance.

### Fable pros

- **Free to start:** Fable offers a free plan, enabling users to create their first interactive demo in just five minutes.

- **Mobile-optimized demos:** Fable ensures that demos are mobile-friendly, providing a seamless experience across all devices.

- **AI demo co-pilot:** Fable’s AI-powered co-pilot, Quilly, helps users generate demo content, customize themes, and build demo modules with ease, all while supporting multiple languages.

- **Demo hubs:** Fable offers advanced demo hub functionality, enabling teams to manage a collection of demos with detailed customization options. Visitors can personalize their demo journey with sophisticated branching features.

### Fable cons

- **Limited user seats:** Fable restricts the number of user seats, which may pose a challenge for larger teams requiring widespread access.

- **Product replication:** Fable creates a replica of your product for demo purposes, limiting direct edits or live product customizations.

## Feature comparison

### Free trial 

- **Fable:** Provides a 14-day free trial for all plans and includes a free plan to create one demo.

- **Supademo:** Offers a free trial for all plans, similar to Fable.

- **Saleo:** Saleo does not offer a free trial.

### AI demos

- **Fable:** Quilly, Fable’s AI demo co-pilot, allows users to create demo content, customize themes, and build modules in various languages using simple prompts.

- **Reprise:** Reprise does not offer AI-powered demos yet.

- **Saleo:** Saleo currently does not support AI-powered demos.

### Engineering effort

- **Fable:** Requires no engineering effort—teams can start creating demos in under five minutes.

- **Reprise:** Requires significant engineering involvement for account setup and demo environments.

- **Saleo:** Setting up Saleo requires substantial engineering effort, as it relies on live product data.

### Type of demos

- **Fable:** Allows users to create fully interactive HTML demos on all plans.

- **Reprise:** Offers options for creating HTML product tours or live environments.

- **Saleo:** Works with live product data, presenting real-time product environments.
