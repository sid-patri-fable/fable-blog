## About Testbox

Testbox is used by sales teams to create custom sandbox environments pre-filled with data for their prospects.

However, since their product works pretty much based on your product’s live instance - significant amount of your engineering team’s time needs to be put in to get this moving. Couple that with a price tag of $38,000/year, it is an expensive option considering the multiple alternatives available in the market today.

If you are on the lookout for a demo platform that is easy to use and can be adopted quickly by your sales team without any engineering effort, do evaluate these Testbox alternatives listed below.

## Alternatives to Testbox

### Fable

**Best suited for**: Marketing & sales teams of all sizes

<FableEmbed src="https://app.sharefable.com/embed/demo/alternatives-demo-8zt47ki0xsfn2oje" />

**Demo capabilities**: Screenshot & HTML demos

**Pros**:

- Start for free: Fable offers a free plan, allowing you to create your first interactive demo in just 5 minutes.

- Demo hubs: Fable has the most advanced demo hub product on the market, enabling teams to create collections of demos with multiple customizations. Visitors can personalize their demo journey with Fable's advanced branching capabilities.

- No engineering support needed: Built to empower marketing & sales teams, Fable ensures no need for engineering involvement to get your account up and running.

**Cons**:

- Limited user seats: Fable does not offer unlimited user seats.

- Replica limitations: It creates a replica of your product and does not support direct edits or customizations to your live product.

**Pricing**: Fable's paid plans start at $40 per user per month.

### Navattic

**Best suited for**: Marketing teams in growing SaaS companies

<img alt="Testbox  alternative - Navattic" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/bbc8da7a-b52d-4f4d-b9b0-c432c8c70bcd"></img>

**Demo capabilities**: Screenshot & HTML demos

**Pros**:

- No engineering required: Marketers can create Navattic demos without engineering resources.

- SEO-friendly demos: Navattic focuses on SEO, making it easier for GTM members to optimize their demos.

- Marketing tool integrations: Navattic integrates seamlessly with Marketo, Mixpanel, Google Analytics, and other tools.

**Cons**:

- No free plan or trial: You must schedule a demo with their sales team to explore Navattic; there is no free option available.

- Lacks demo hub: Navattic does not offer a demo center feature.

- Steep learning curve: Starting with Navattic can be more complex compared to other platforms.

**Pricing**: Begins at $500 per month, billed annually.

### Storylane

**Best suited for**: Sales and marketing teams in growing SaaS companies

<img alt="Testbox alternative - Storylane" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/9b109d1f-c593-4b69-add6-f9baacabd2c5"></img>

**Demo capabilities**: Screenshot & HTML demos

**Pros**:

- Versatile demo options: Teams can select between screenshot and HTML capture demos based on their requirements.

- Quick setup: You can get started promptly without developer involvement.

- Comprehensive integrations: Integrates with HubSpot, Segment, Google Analytics, and more.

**Cons**:

- Minimum user requirement: HTML capture demos require a minimum of 5 licenses.

- No device segmentation: Storylane does not offer viewer segmentation by device.

- Limited seats: No option for unlimited user seats.

**Pricing**: Starts at $500 per month, billed annually.

### Reprise

**Best suited for**: Sales teams in large enterprises
