## What is a product tour and why is it useful?
Product tour software is a tool that allows businesses to create interactive guides and product walkthroughs to assist users during the onboarding process. This software enables companies to offer contextual in-app guidance, improving the overall user experience, and increasing customer engagement as well as customer satisfaction.

One of the key benefits of the product tour tool is its ability to simplify the creation of guides for even the most complex product features without requiring extensive coding knowledge. This saves time and resources for engineering teams, as they can focus on other important tasks instead of spending countless hours building and maintaining these guides. Additionally, product tour tools empower product teams, including product managers and marketers, by giving them more control over the design and appearance of the onboarding process. They can easily customize the guides based on their brand identity, ensuring a consistent and seamless user experience from start to finish.

Product tour software also assists in personalizing your user journey with contextual guidance, an onboarding checklist, advanced analytics, and multiple integration options. With their in-app walkthroughs, product tour software helps in increasing your product adoption rates.

Product tour software solutions are beneficial for businesses, especially SaaS companies, as they help improve the onboarding process. This software allows for the creation of interactive guides and provides greater control over design elements for product teams. By utilizing this software, companies can enhance user engagement, convert new customers into active users, and ultimately drive business growth.

In addition to feature adoption and onboarding, product tour tools also allow you to:

1. Improve employee onboarding and ongoing training for new product features.
2. Reduce customer support tickets by including interactive demos and interactive product tours in your knowledge base.
