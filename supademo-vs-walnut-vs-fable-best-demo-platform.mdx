## Supademo

<img alt="Supademo" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/a629387d-3741-4c1c-8912-afa7580b85de"></img>

Supademo provides a streamlined platform for creating product demos using screenshots, primarily focused on improving product onboarding, training, and customer support. The platform also incorporates AI-powered voiceovers to make demos more engaging, and they are perfect for embedding on websites or sharing with clients.

### Supademo Pros

- **AI Integration:** Supademo leverages AI for voiceovers and automated text generation, making demos more dynamic and polished for users.

- **Ease of use:** With a user-friendly interface, Supademo allows for quick and effortless demo creation, without needing a steep learning curve.

- **Demo branching:** The platform allows for demo branching, enabling users to personalize the experience and make demos more engaging and interactive.

### Supademo Cons

- **Limited flexibility:** Supademo only supports screenshot-based demos, limiting options for more interactive or dynamic content.

- **Limited integrations:** Lacks integration with some widely-used marketing tools such as Marketo or Segment, which can be a drawback for marketing teams needing greater connectivity.

- **Minimal customizations:** Supademo doesn’t capture or utilize HTML and CSS elements, limiting customization options.

### Exploring alternatives to Supademo

With the lack of HTML demo features and limited customization, some users may find themselves exploring [other platforms](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/supademo-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=supademo-vs-walnut-vs-fable-best-demo-platform) to better meet their go-to-market needs.

## Walnut

<img alt="Walnut" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/b97dbf32-27ab-428e-922d-dba6b08d5af4"></img>

Walnut is a demo platform built for sales teams, allowing them to create interactive, clickable demos for use in live presentations or as follow-ups. The no-code interface ensures that users can easily customize demos, making the sales process more engaging and seamless.

### Walnut Pros

- **Collaborate with prospects:** Walnut allows prospects to comment and collaborate directly within demos, fostering interaction and engagement.

- **Advanced features:** Walnut includes robust features that help sales teams craft highly personalized demos to suit the needs of individual prospects.

- **Demo templates:** The platform offers reusable demo templates, making it easier for sales reps to quickly personalize and tailor demos for different prospects.

### Walnut Cons

- **No free trial:** Walnut does not provide a free trial option, requiring users to commit before experiencing the platform.

- **Unnecessary complexity:** For users needing simpler demos, Walnut can become overly complicated.

- **Steep learning curve:** Walnut’s extensive features result in a steeper learning curve, making it harder for beginners to get started immediately.

### Exploring alternatives to Walnut

Given the lack of a free trial, slower implementation, and complex features, teams may consider [alternatives](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/walnut-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=supademo-vs-walnut-vs-fable-best-demo-platform) that provide simpler setups and greater flexibility, particularly as Walnut’s pricing starts at $10,000 per year.

## Fable

Fable is an interactive demo platform aimed at marketing and sales teams of all sizes, offering extensive customization to create demos that enhance engagement and streamline sales. With advanced analytics and seamless integration with other tools, Fable helps improve sales strategy and performance.

### Fable Pros

- **Free trial:** Fable offers a free plan, allowing users to create their first interactive demo in just five minutes, making it easy to get started.

- **Extensive customization:** With robust HTML customization options, Fable allows teams to modify, hide, blur, or remove elements within demos, ensuring they can adapt demos to meet their specific needs.

- **Multiple integrations:** Fable integrates smoothly with popular tools like HubSpot, Salesforce, Mailchimp, and Pipedrive, ensuring that demo data is seamlessly managed within your marketing and sales workflows.

### Fable Cons

- **Limited user capacity:** Fable does not provide unlimited user seats, which could be restrictive for larger teams needing broad access.

- **Product duplication:** The platform creates a replica of your product for demo purposes, which may limit the ability to make live edits or customizations in real-time.
