## Arcade

<img alt="Arcade" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/d3c4d93c-3436-44ee-aa63-ef963e2543c9"></img>

Arcade is a simple demo platform designed for teams that need to create video demos using screenshots. It’s a user-friendly solution, ideal for producing basic video content for onboarding and training purposes. Arcade is often compared to Loom for its straightforward approach to video creation.

### Arcade pros

- **Multimedia support:** Arcade allows users to enhance demos by adding audio, background music, and images, making them more engaging for viewers.

- **AI-generated content:** The platform helps speed up demo creation by utilizing AI to generate written content for videos.

- **Quick creation:** Arcade enables teams to quickly produce demos and training materials, offering a time-efficient alternative to traditional video production methods.

### Arcade cons

- **Lacks interactivity:** Arcade supports only video-based demos, making it less suited for teams that need interactive, HTML-based demos.

- **Limited customization:** While easy to use, Arcade offers fewer advanced customization and post-production editing options.

- **Basic functionality:** The platform focuses on video creation, limiting its flexibility for creating demos with interactive elements like HTML.

### Exploring alternatives to Arcade

Due to its basic functionality and lack of advanced features, teams might consider [alternative platforms](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/arcade-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=arcade-vs-demoboost-vs-fable-best-demo-platform) that offer greater interactivity and customization for more complex needs.

## Demoboost

<img alt="Demoboost" src="https://documentden-deployments.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/public/48a64de9-3499-4776-a334-470758559c58"></img>

Demoboost is a powerful demo automation platform designed for pre-sales teams. It facilitates the creation and management of interactive demos and offers features like speaker notes and real-time collaboration, making it ideal for live presentations.

### Demoboost pros

- **Advanced live demo tools:** Demoboost includes collaboration tools and speaker notes, enhancing the quality of live demos and presentations.

- **Extensive integrations:** The platform integrates with key tools like HubSpot, Clearbit, Slack, and Google Analytics, streamlining demo tracking and management.

- **Collaboration-friendly:** Teams can collaborate in real time during demo creation, ensuring high-quality, consistent presentations.

### Demoboost cons

- **Steep learning curve:** Customizing demos on Demoboost often requires coding skills, which can be challenging for teams without technical expertise.

- **Performance issues:** Some users report performance slowdowns, especially when working with larger or more complex demos.

- **Time-consuming setup:** Initial configuration, including setting up variables and organizing demo screens, can be lengthy, though the long-term benefits are worthwhile.

### Exploring alternatives to Demoboost

With a starting price of $10,000 and no free trial, Demoboost’s complexity and performance issues may encourage teams to consider [other platforms](https://www.sharefable.com/blog/demoboost-alternatives-and-competitors-in-2024?ref=arcade-vs-demoboost-vs-fable-best-demo-platform) that offer simpler setups and more accessible pricing.

## Fable

Fable is a robust platform designed for marketing and sales teams of any size. It provides highly customizable interactive demos that improve engagement and streamline sales processes. Fable’s advanced analytics and seamless integrations make it a standout option for teams looking to elevate their sales and customer engagement strategies.

### Fable pros

- **Free to start:** Fable offers a free plan, allowing users to create their first interactive demo in under five minutes.

- **Mobile-optimized demos:** Fable ensures that all demos are fully optimized for mobile devices, delivering a smooth, responsive experience on any platform.

- **Advanced demo hubs:** Fable’s demo hubs allow teams to manage collections of demos with extensive customization, letting prospects navigate personalized demo journeys.

- **AI-powered co-pilot:** Fable’s AI co-pilot, Quilly, assists in quickly creating demo content, supports multiple languages, and helps customize themes using simple prompts.

### Fable cons

- **User seat limitations:** Fable restricts the number of user seats, which may create challenges for larger teams needing broader access.

- **Demo creation limitations:** Fable replicates your product for demo purposes, meaning you can’t edit the live product directly during demos.

- **Fable:** Fully optimized for all devices, ensuring demos run smoothly on both mobile and desktop platforms.
